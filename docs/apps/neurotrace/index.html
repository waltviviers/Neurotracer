// HOW TO PLAY

DECODE THE SEQUENCE

Each round, the grid lights up a sequence of tiles. Watch carefully — then tap them back in the exact same order. Miss one and you lose a life. Survive long enough and the grid grows deeper.

01

MEMORIZE

Watch the tiles flash in sequence. Every round adds more tiles and grows the grid.

02

REPLAY

Tap the tiles back in the same order — perfectly. One wrong tap costs a life.

03

SURVIVE

Earn replay tokens to re-watch sequences. Collect 13 to continue after a game over.

04

FREE THEM

Clear all 27 rounds and liberate the souls trapped in the neural network.
